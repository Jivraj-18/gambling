## Questions to Ask player, during play 

1. Who all seem to bluffing
2. How sure are you that your hand will win
3. With this hand how much are you willing to raise
4. After reaching bets in pt.3 will you bluff or fold
5. Which of these feelings are overpowered for you rn. Fear hope greed (or none)
6. If you lose, how big of an impact will it be
7. What are you feeling. Is this good, ok ok, or bad for you.


## Questions to be asked after playing game : 
After the end you can ask at what point things became bad for you, where should you have done things differently.
